Payment Options

Finnish customers

  • Invoices are sent monthly by e-mail, and paid using a Finnish bank transaction.
  • Invoices include current Finnish VAT.

Customers within EU

  • Invoices are sent monthly by e-mail, and paid using PayPal. PayPal accepts direct VISA and other credit card payments, and does not require you to register with PayPal.
  • If you provide a valid EU VAT ID, invoices do not include VAT (but you must pay them according to EU VAT laws). If you don't provide a valid EU VAT ID, invoices include current Finnish VAT.

Customers outside EU

  • Invoices are sent monthly by e-mail, and paid using PayPal. PayPal accepts direct VISA and other credit card payments, and does not require you to register with PayPal.
  • Invoices do not include VAT, you must pay VAT and other relevant taxes according to your local laws. If the information you provide is incorrect, we reserve the right to add the current Finnish VAT to the invoices.
