Partner Process

How to Become a Partner?

To become a partner, you need to:

  • Accept the Partner License Agreement (PLA) and other legal obligations
  • Fill in and send a Request Partnership form
  • Receive a confirmation from the sales staff

Floating Licenses and License Activation

To make service installation and activation as convenient as possible for both installation and service provider partners, partners are supplied beforehand with "floating licenses". Floating licenses are directly functional but not yet associated with a specific customer. They allow installation and activation without a license request delay.

When you install a new server, use one of the (unused) floating license keys during installation. Then use the Activate License page to activate that license from floating to active mode. License keys must be activated within 7 days of installation. Unactivated floating licenses will be cancelled after 7 days of installation.

support will provide you with more floating license keys as you start to exhaust existing ones. If you need more floating license keys before that, please contact support.

Process for Service Provider Partners

There are two main processes for service provider partners. First, partners install and activate servers using floating licenses and activate the floating licenses within 7 days of installation. Second, invoices the partners after a month is through for the licenses used during the past month.

The monthly fee is computed as follows: the collective amount of license fees excluding VAT for the partner's licenses, minus the partner discount, plus VAT (if applicable). will send an invoice for this amount through e-mail (or as agreed) after a month is through. The invoice is due 14th of the month following the invoicing period, and is paid using a Paypal payment (see Payment Options).

The collective monthly license fees are computed as a sum of individual license fees. License fees are described in Pricing. In particular, the highest fee for a month determines the license fee for the month.

Invoicing example:

  • Customer license fees for January 2008 are 7000€ (excluding VAT)
  • Partner discount is 1275€, leaving a total discounted price of 5725€
  • Total sum is 5725€ + VAT (if applicable), due February 14th, 2008

See Payment Options for VAT details.
