Partner Benefits

For Service Provider Partners

  • Service Pricing - partner can price the service to their customers without restrictions. licenses have no start-up fee, minimizing partner and customer risk.
  • Partner discount - partner gets a percentual discount based on aggregated total monthly invoicing. Partner invoice is based on standard pricing minus the partner discount.
  • Technical support - provides technical support to ensure service provisioning goes as smoothly as possible.
  • Minimal support costs - because is easy to install and does not require client software installation (leading to dramatically fewer software compatibility issues), costly on-site customer support is minimized.
  • Sales Materials - we provide our partners with sales materials to help in their customer communications.
  • Floating licenses - instantly usable pre-generated license keys allow customer service reactions without delay. Floating licenses are activated promptly after service has been taken into use.

For Installation Partners

  • Installation Service Pricing - partner can price installation service without restrictions. licenses have no start-up fee, minimizing partner and customer risk.
  • Technical support - provides technical support to ensure customer installations go as smoothly as possible.
  • Easy installation - because is easy to install and take into use (server installation and client configuration is possible in less than an hour), installation service throughput is improved. Because there are no client software installations, there are minimal software compatibility issues to deal with in customer premises.
  • Sales Materials - we provide our partners with sales materials to help in their customer communications.
  • Floating licenses - instantly usable pre-generated license keys allow customer service installation without delay. Floating licenses are activated promptly after service has been taken into use.
