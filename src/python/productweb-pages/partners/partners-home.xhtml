Partners

We are committed to mutually beneficial distribution partnerships around the world and are looking for partners who wish to solve their customer's connectivity problems in an easy and secure manner. We provide our partners with technical support, product information, sales materials, floating license keys, and a way to benefit financially from the cost effectiveness of .

There are two partnership categories:

  • Service provider partners - provide installation, invoicing, and technical support for their customers. Service provider partners are allowed to price their licensing without restrictions, and are awarded a partner discount based on monthly invoicing volume.
  • Installation partners - provide installation for their customers, but don't provide invoicing or technical support. Installation partners are allowed to price their installation services without restrictions.

See partner documentation to find out more about partnership. You can also send us a question if you don't find the information you're looking for.
