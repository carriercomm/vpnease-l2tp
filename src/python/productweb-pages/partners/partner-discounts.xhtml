Partner Discounts For Service Providers

The service provider partner discount is based on the collective monthly amount of product license fees for the partner's customers, measured in euros excluding VAT. The discount structure is cumulative:

Monthly fee limit Discount for fees exceeding limit Cumulative discount for fees below limit
500€ 10% 0€
1000€ 15% 50€
2500€ 20% 275€
5000€ 25% 775€
10000€ 30% 2025€

Example: for collective monthly license fees of 7000€ (excluding VAT), the discount is computed as follows:

  • First 500 euros: no discount
  • Next 500 euros (500 to 1000 euros): 50€
  • Next 1500 euros (1000 to 2500 euros): 225€
  • Next 2500 euros (2500 to 5000 euros): 500€
  • Next 2000 euros (5000 to 7000 euros): 500€
  • Total discount: (50 + 225 + 500 + 500) euros = 1275€

If applicable, VAT is added to final partner fee after discount has been subtracted. See Payment Options for regional VAT details.
